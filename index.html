1. Cardiologist

Specialty:

Cardiology focuses on heart-related conditions and diseases. Cardiologists specialize in diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart disorders, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects.

Services Offered:

  • Evaluation and management of chest pain and heart palpitations
  • Cardiac imaging (echocardiograms, stress tests, etc.)
  • Treatment for high blood pressure and cholesterol


2. Pulmonologist:

Specialty:

Pulmonology deals with respiratory system diseases. Pulmonologists diagnose and manage conditions affecting the lungs and respiratory tract, such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), pneumonia, and lung cancer.

Services Offered:

  • Diagnosis and treatment of breathing difficulties and lung diseases
  • Management of chronic cough, wheezing, and shortness of breath
  • Pulmonary function tests and bronchoscopy


3. Dermatologist:

Specialty:

Dermatology focuses on skin, hair, nail, and mucous membrane disorders. Dermatologists diagnose and treat various skin conditions, including acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin cancer, and allergic reactions.

Services Offered:

  • Skin examinations and treatment for various skin disorders
  • Cosmetic procedures like Botox, fillers, and laser therapy
  • Skin cancer screenings and surgical removal


4. Gastroenterologist:

Specialty:

Gastroenterology involves the digestive system and its disorders. Gastroenterologists diagnose and treat conditions affecting the stomach, intestines, liver, and pancreas, such as acid reflux, ulcers, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), and liver diseases.

Services Offered:

  • Diagnosis and management of digestive disorders
  • Endoscopic procedures (colonoscopy, endoscopy, etc.)
  • Liver disease diagnosis, management, and treatment
